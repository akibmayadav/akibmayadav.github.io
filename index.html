VISUALIZING HOUSE
OWNERSHIP TRENDS IN
CALIFORNIA
In the year 2000, the population
of California was 33.99 Million
Were there enough houses in California?
How many more houses need to be build
for the growing population?
The population in California has
five major ethnic groups : Whites
Blacks, Latinos, Asians, and Others
Different ethnic groups have different
houseownership rates, with a section
who don't own houses.
On the whole this leads to a high percentage
of the population without an owned house.
This number has been increasing and is
predicted to increase in the future.
The resulting houseownership rate of
ethnic groups indicates a major lot of non house owners come
from the MINORITIES.
This decrease in house ownership rate is a result of
unproportional growth of
population of ethnic groups.
The percentage of population
owning a house is expected to decrease
from 57.1% in 2000 to 44.2% in 2040.

MATDAN
Informing the Electorate



India is considered the largest democracy in world because of the sheer population size. Given the complex dynamics of the Indian electorate, it is not the most ideal democracy. A well informed and educated electorate would catalyze India's transformation from a flawed to a realistic democracy. Matdan aims to help transition this flawed democracy towards a more realistic one by informing the electorate. It is designed for the upcoming GUJARAT LEGISLATIVE ELECTIONS 2018.

Matdan brings you interactive visualizations of socio-economic parameter data, historical political data and upcoming election candidate affidavit data to better understand the political landscape of your state.

Visualizing Affidavit Data of Candidates contesting in Gujarat 2017 Legislative Elections
